W3C HTML Validator

The W3C HTML Validator checks your HTML for standard compliance and gives clear error messages that help you fix your code. It identifies common issues such as missing closing tags, incorrect nesting, and invalid attributes. The validator also explains where the error occurs, making it easier to locate and correct mistakes. Using this tool helped me improve the structure and reliability of my webpages. Overall, it reinforced the importance of writing clean, standards-compliant HTML that works consistently across browsers.
